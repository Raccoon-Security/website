Frequently Asked Questions about Security

What is cyber security?

Cyber security is how individuals and organisations reduce the risk of cyber attack.

Why is cyber security important?

Ensuring cyber security is crucial to protect sensitive information, maintain customer trust, prevent financial losses, and avoid damage to reputation.
